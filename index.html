Bojan Lazarov

Actor and Assistant Professor at Faculty of Dramatic Arts, University of Ss. Cyril and Methodius

Graduated actor from Faculty of Dramatic Arts, at the University of Ss. Cyril and Methodius in Skopje , Republic of Macedonia. In my acting career I have 19 theater shows, performances and movies. "Class Enemy", "Eyewitness", "The Glass Menagerie", "A Midsummer night's dream", "The Pelican", "Towards myself" are just a part of the shows that I took part.
A few months after graduation, I was called to work with Professor Krenare Nevzati and to be part of the Faculty of Dramatic Arts as Demonstrator for the subject Stage Movements and dances. In December, 2016 I got my Master's degree from the Faculty of Music Arts, at the University of Ss. Cyril and Methodius in Skopje, Republic of Macedonia.
